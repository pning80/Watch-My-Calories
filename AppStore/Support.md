## Troubleshooting

### Food analysis isn't working
- Ensure your device has an active internet connection. Food photos are sent to a server for AI analysis and require connectivity.
- Try taking a clearer, well-lit photo of your food.

### Camera permission denied
- Go to **Settings > Calorie Watcher > Camera** on your device and enable camera access.

### HealthKit data not showing
- Go to **Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Calorie Watcher** and ensure Active Energy is enabled.
- HealthKit is optional — the app works without it, but your calorie goal won't adjust for activity.

### Deleting your data
- Delete individual food entries or entire meal groups from within the app.
- To remove all data, uninstall the app. All data is stored on your device only — nothing is stored on our servers.
